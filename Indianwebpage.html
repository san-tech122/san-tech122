Must-Visit Places in India

Jaipur, Rajasthan

Pink City, Rajasthan

Known as the Pink City, Jaipur is famous for its stunning palaces, vibrant bazaars, and rich cultural heritage.

Recommended Stay:

Rambagh Palace - Former royal residence turned luxury hotel with exquisite Rajasthani architecture.

Kerala Backwaters

Alleppey, Kerala

A network of tranquil canals, lakes and lagoons where you can experience Kerala's serene beauty on a houseboat.

Recommended Stay:

Punnamada Resort - Luxury houseboats with traditional Keralite decor and authentic local cuisine.

Taj Mahal, Agra

Agra, Uttar Pradesh

The iconic white marble mausoleum, one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a symbol of eternal love.

Recommended Stay:

The Oberoi Amarvilas - Luxury hotel with uninterrupted views of the Taj Mahal from every room.
